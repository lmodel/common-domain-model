    Enum: PartyRoleEnum

    name: PartyRoleEnum
description: The enumerated values for the party role. The enumerated values go beyond
  the FpML partyRoleScheme as they also include elements that are part of the FpML
  Trade, such as the Barrier Determination Agent and the Hedging Party.
from_schema: https://w3id.org/lmodel/common-domain-model
exact_mappings:
- http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role
related_mappings:
- oscal:Role
rank: 1000
permissible_values:
  Accountant:
    text: Accountant
    description: Organization responsible for preparing the accounting for the trade.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#Accountant
  AgentLender:
    text: AgentLender
    description: An agent who lends securities of its principals under stock lending
      arrangements.
  AllocationAgent:
    text: AllocationAgent
    description: The organization responsible for supplying the allocations for a
      trade to be allocated to multiple accounts/organizations.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#AllocationAgent
  ArrangingBroker:
    text: ArrangingBroker
    description: 'The organization that arranged the trade, i.e. brought together
      the counterparties.  Synonyms/Alternatives: Inter-dealer broker, agent.'
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#ArrangingBroker
  BarrierDeterminationAgent:
    text: BarrierDeterminationAgent
    description: The party specified in the related confirmation as Barrier Determination
      Agent.
  BeneficialOwner:
    text: BeneficialOwner
    description: The party that lends out securities under stock lending arrangements
      via an Agent Lender.
  Beneficiary:
    text: Beneficiary
    description: Organization that suffers the economic benefit of the trade.  The
      beneficiary may be distinct from the principal/counterparty - an example occurs
      when a hedge fund trades via a prime broker; in this case the principal is the
      prime broker, but the beneficiary is the hedge fund.  This can be represented
      as a payer/receiver account in the name of the hedge fund, but it is also possible
      to add the party role of 'Beneficiary' at the partyTradeInformation level.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#Beneficiary
  BookingParty:
    text: BookingParty
    description: The entity for which the organization supporting the trade's processing
      has booked/recorded the trade. This is used in non-reporting workflows situations
      in which the trade doesn\'t need to be reported but a firm still wants to specify
      their own side.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#BookingParty
  Borrower:
    text: Borrower
    description: The party that borrows securities under stock lending arrangements.
  Buyer:
    text: Buyer
    description: Acquirer of the legal title to the financial instrument. In the case
      of an option, the buyer is the holder of the option. In the case of a swap or
      forward, the buyer will be determined by industry best practice.  This does
      not refer to an investor or investment manager or other organization on what
      is typically called  the 'Buy side'; for that, see the 'Client' role. Corresponds
      to 'Buyer' as defined in certain regulations such as ESMA MiFID II/MIFIR RTS
      22 field 9.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#Buyer
  BuyerDecisionMaker:
    text: BuyerDecisionMaker
    description: The party or person who, having legal authority to act on behalf
      of the trade counterparty acting as Buyer as defined in this coding scheme,
      made the decision to acquire the financial instrument. Corresponds to 'buyer
      decision maker' as defined in ESMA\'s MIFIR RTS 23 report. This does not refer
      to the decision maker for what is traditionally called the 'Buy side'; for that,
      see the 'Client Decision Maker' role.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#BuyerDecisionMaker
  Chargor:
    text: Chargor
    description: The party that provides credit support under English Law.
  ClearingClient:
    text: ClearingClient
    description: An organization that clears trades through a clearing house, via
      a clearing broker (member of the clearing house) who acts as an agent on its
      behalf. The term 'client' refers to the organization\'s role in the clearing
      process in relation to its clearing broker, and not whether it is a price maker
      or taker in the execution process.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#ClearingClient
  ClearingExceptionParty:
    text: ClearingExceptionParty
    description: A party to the trade that claims a clearing exception, such as an
      end-user exception under Dodd-Frank Act provisions.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#ClearingExceptionParty
  ClearingFirm:
    text: ClearingFirm
    description: 'Organization that submits the trade to a clearing house on behalf
      of the principal. Synonyms/alternates: Futures Commission Merchant (FCM), Clearing
      Broker, Clearing Member Firm. Some implementations use ''Clearing Broker'' as
      synonym.'
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#ClearingFirm
  ClearingOrganization:
    text: ClearingOrganization
    description: The organization that acts as a central counterparty to clear a derivatives
      contract.  This is used to represent the role of Central Counterparties (CCPs)
      or Derivative Clearing Organizations (DCOs).  Sometimes called 'ClearingService'.
      Some implementations also use the term 'Clearer'.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#ClearingOrganization
  Client:
    text: Client
    description: Client as defined under ESMA MIFIR. This is generally the investor
      or other client of an investment firm, and is synonymous with the Beneficiary
      in many circumstances.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#Client
  ClientDecisionMaker:
    text: ClientDecisionMaker
    description: The party or person who, having legal authority to act on behalf
      of a trade counterparty, made the decision to acquire or sell the financial
      instrument.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#ClientDecisionMaker
  ConfirmationPlatform:
    text: ConfirmationPlatform
    description: Organization serving as a financial intermediary for the purposes
      of electronic confirmation or providing services for post-processing of transactional
      data.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#ConfirmationPlatform
  ContractualParty:
    text: ContractualParty
    description: A party to a contractual document.  If the intended usage relates
      to the context of the trade lifecycle, more specific annotations have been defined
      which might be more appropriate.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#ContractualParty
  CounterPartyAffiliate:
    text: CounterPartyAffiliate
    description: Organization officially attached to the counterparty. e.g. partner,
      branch, subsidiary.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#CounterPartyAffiliate
  CounterPartyUltimateParent:
    text: CounterPartyUltimateParent
    description: The topmost entity or organization, within the corporate hierarchy,
      responsible for the reporting party.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#CounterPartyUltimateParent
  Counterparty:
    text: Counterparty
    description: 'An economic counterparty to the trade. Synonym: principal.'
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#Counterparty
  CreditSupportProvider:
    text: CreditSupportProvider
    description: Organization that enhances the credit of another organization (similar
      to guarantor, but may not fully guarantee the obligation).
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#CreditSupportProvider
  Custodian:
    text: Custodian
    description: Organization that maintains custody of the asset represented by the
      trade on behalf of the owner/principal.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#Custodian
  DataSubmitter:
    text: DataSubmitter
    description: Entity submitting the transaction report to the competent authority.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#DataSubmitter
  DeterminingParty:
    text: DeterminingParty
    description: The party referenced is specified in the contract confirmation as
      Determination Party.
  DisputingParty:
    text: DisputingParty
    description: Organization that is disputing the trade or transaction.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#DisputingParty
  DocumentRepository:
    text: DocumentRepository
    description: A marketplace organization which purpose is to maintain document
      records.  If the intended usage relates to the context of the trade lifecycle,
      more specific annotations have been defined which might be more appropriate.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#DocumentRepository
  ExecutingBroker:
    text: ExecutingBroker
    description: The (generally sell-side) organization that executed the trade; the
      price-making party.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#ExecutingBroker
  ExecutingEntity:
    text: ExecutingEntity
    description: Entity executing the transaction.  If the transaction is executed
      directly by the reporting party, it will be the reporting party.  If it is executed
      by an execution agent or an affiliated party on behalf of the reporting party,
      it will be that affiliate or agent.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#ExecutingEntity
  ExecutionAgent:
    text: ExecutionAgent
    description: The (generally buy-side) organization that acts to execute trades
      on behalf of an investor. Typically this is an investment manager or asset manager,
      and also makes the investment decisions for the investor. If required, a separate
      InvestmentDecision role can be specified to distinguish that the party making
      the investment decision is different.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#ExecutionAgent
  ExecutionFacility:
    text: ExecutionFacility
    description: 'The facility, exchange, or market where the trade was executed.
      Synonym: Swap Execution Facility, Designated Contract Market, Execution Venue.'
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#ExecutionFacility
  Guarantor:
    text: Guarantor
    description: Organization that backs (guarantees) the credit risk of the trade.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#Guarantor
  HedgingParty:
    text: HedgingParty
    description: The ISDA Hedging Party that is specified in the related confirmation
      as Hedging, or if no Hedging Party is specified, either party to the contract.
  Lender:
    text: Lender
    description: The party that lends out securities under stock lending arrangements
      as principal.
  MarginAffiliate:
    text: MarginAffiliate
    description: Margin affiliate as defined by U.S. margin and capital rules §23.151.
  OrderTransmitter:
    text: OrderTransmitter
    description: 'The entity transmitting the order to the reporting firm. Synonym:
      Transmitting Firm.'
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#OrderTransmitter
  Pledgor:
    text: Pledgor
    description: The party that provides credit support under New York Law.
  PrimeBroker:
    text: PrimeBroker
    description: The organization that takes on or took on the credit risk for this
      trade by stepping in between the two economic parties (without a central counterparty
      clearing mechanism).
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#PrimeBroker
  PriorTradeRepository:
    text: PriorTradeRepository
    description: The trade repository at which the trade was reported previous to
      the current trade repository.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#PriorTradeRepository
  PTRRServiceProvider:
    text: PTRRServiceProvider
    description: The party that acts as either a portfolio compression or portfolio
      rebalancing service provider, as defined under ESMA MIFIR
  PublicationVenue:
    text: PublicationVenue
    description: The reporting service (whether trade repository, market data service,
      or exchange/facility/venue data distribution service) that published the report
      of this trade.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#PublicationVenue
  ReportingParty:
    text: ReportingParty
    description: The party with the regulatory responsibility to report this trade.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#ReportingParty
  ReportingPartyAffiliate:
    text: ReportingPartyAffiliate
    description: Organization officially attached to the reporting party  e.g. partner,
      branch, subsidiary.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#ReportingPartyAffiliate
  ReportingPartyUltimateParent:
    text: ReportingPartyUltimateParent
    description: The topmost entity or organization, within the corporate hierarchy,
      responsible for the reporting party.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#ReportingPartyUltimateParent
  Seller:
    text: Seller
    description: 'A counterparty in a trade, which performs in one of the following
      capacities: 1) it transfers or agrees to transfer in the future an instrument
      or title to that instrument in exchange for payment, 2) it writes a derivatives
      instrument such as an option or a swap in which it provides risk protection
      to the buyer. This does not refer to the broker/dealer or other organization
      on what is typically called  the ''Sell side''; for that, see the ''Executing
      Broker'' role. Corresponds to ''Seller'' as defined in certain regulations such
      as ESMA MiFID II/MIFIR RTS 22 field 16.'
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#Seller
  SellerDecisionMaker:
    text: SellerDecisionMaker
    description: The party or person who, having legal authority to act on behalf
      of the trade counterparty acting as Seller as defined in this coding scheme,
      made the decision to sell the financial instrument. Corresponds to 'seller decision
      maker' as defined in ESMA\'s MIFIR RTS 23 report. This does not refer to the
      decision maker for what is traditionally called the 'Sell side'; for that, see
      the 'Trader' person role.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#SellerDecisionMaker
  SecuredParty:
    text: SecuredParty
    description: The party that receives credit support under New York or English
      Law.
  SettlementAgent:
    text: SettlementAgent
    description: The organization that makes or receives payments on behalf of the
      given principal party.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#SettlementAgent
  TradeRepository:
    text: TradeRepository
    description: An organization that maintains records of the trade for regulatory
      reporting purposes.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#TradeRepository
  TradeSource:
    text: TradeSource
    description: The organization that originally supplied the record of the trade.
      In the context of regulatory reporting, it is the submitter of the trade record
      to a regulator or TR.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#TradeSource
  TradingManager:
    text: TradingManager
    description: 'The entity responsible for managing the assets/investments of this
      party.  Synonym:  Asset Manager, Investment Manager, Trading Advisory.'
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#TradingManager
  TradingPartner:
    text: TradingPartner
    description: An entity with which this party trades from time to time, ie. with
      which it acts as a counterparty on some transactions.   This role is used for
      static reference data, not individual transactions.
    meaning: http://www.fpml.org/coding-scheme/party-role-4-0#TradingPartner
  TripartyAgent:
    text: TripartyAgent
    description: A third party entity that acts as an intermediary and provides automated
      clearing, settlement, allocation, valuation and reporting services.
  ThirdPartyCustodian:
    text: ThirdPartyCustodian
    description: A third-party providing custody, settlement, segregation and reporting
      services.
